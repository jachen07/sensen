森森和乖乖的约定

核心价值：平等，尊重，真诚，独立

核心承诺

  • No cheating at all — relationship ends immediately if it happens
  • Honest communication if either of us meets someone new
  • Be serious, exclusive, and part of each other’s lives
  • Each of us has three veto votes per year to end any disagreement
  • Grow together and support each other toward better habits and a brighter future
  • Respect each other and allow them to spend time with friends and family

日常相处

  • 每天说“早安”， “晚安”，“我爱你”，和“我喜欢你”
  • 每天提醒对方“I like you” & “I love you”
  • 每周至少一起吃一次饭（如果在同一城市）
  • 每周抽出时间一起度过（看电影、运动、做饭等）
  • 如同城，尽可能每天见面
  • 每天都要聊天和相处 至少每天早中晚都要发消息
  • 对方生病时候 必须要关心照顾ta
  • 一定要鼓励和支持对方成为更好的人 特别是他们内耗或者有烦心事
  • 要给对方准备小惊喜

异地恋相处指南

  • 优先级：视频通话 > 语音通话 > 语音消息 > 照片 > 发消息
  • 无需即时回复，但要告诉对方为什么回复的慢
  • 让对方知道你在做什么，并描述所见所闻
  • 让对方知道距离不是问题 我们的future都有对方的存在
  • 要给对方拍照 带纪念品回来 然后要视频

解决冲突

  • We're on the same team. It's you and me vs the problem, not me vs you.
  • Let me know if you want to vent or you want advice
  • Never go to bed angry or upset at each other
  • Try to resolve the conflict on the day, don't leave it for the future
  • If you are upset, please communicate and don't close yourself, tell the other party how to resolve the issue and make it up to you
  • Don't give cold shoulder (冷战), respond to each other even if we are angry (within 6 hrs)
  • When one of us is angry, take a deep breath before responding with words, actions, or emotions (there is no takebacks!!!)
  • Consider things from each other’s perspectives and give 台阶
  • Once a month we will have check-in to see if there's anything that we can change for the better and any discomfort (only when face to face)

隐私与界限

  • 不向朋友或者在网络上过度分享关系细节
  • 公共场合保持距离，像朋友一样相处（可拥抱但不可以亲亲）
